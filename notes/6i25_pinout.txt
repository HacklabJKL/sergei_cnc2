2 ports of 17 IO pins each.

Port 1:
- 5 pins X: FWD/REV/FAULT/A/B
- 5 pins Y: FWD/REV/FAULT/A/B
- 5 pins Z: FWD/REV/FAULT/A/B
- HCURR output


Port 2:
- 5 pins A: FWD/REV/FAULT/A/B
- 2 pins 48V: ON/OK
- 1 pin SPINDLE_ON
- 1 pin TOOL_SENSOR
- 1 pin PROBE
- 3 pins X/Y/Z home
